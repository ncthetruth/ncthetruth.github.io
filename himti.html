HIMTI

Association of Computer Science Students at Bina Nusantara University

HIMTI is a student association of computer science tasked with unifying and developing all computer science students at Bina Nusantara University to make HIMTI BINUS University known as a student association recognized throughout Southeast Asia by 2025. It aims to strengthen the bonds among HIMTI family members through knowledge, experience, and values, forming a solid HIMTI family. The organization seeks to instill and develop a professional attitude in each individual within HIMTI, preparing them to face the challenges of the industrial world, careers, and global environmental development. Additionally, HIMTI upholds a strong sense of nationalism, demonstrating its commitment to serving Indonesia.

Activities Performed

  • HIMTI LDKP 2023

    • HIMTI LDKP 2023 is an inter-student organization event designed to provide training on leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. The objective of HIMTI LDKP 2023 is not only to impart essential skills but also to strengthen the bond among its members. The event includes activities such as focus group discussions aimed at sharpening critical thinking, dealing with diverse perspectives, defending arguments, and enhancing public speaking abilities.

    I served dual roles as a member of the DnD team and as the PIC. In the DnD capacity, my responsibilities included generating ideas and presenting them to HIMTI members, evaluating their suitability to meet the needs of HIMTI LDKP 2023. Additionally, I undertook the documentation of all activities throughout the HIMTI LDKP 2023 event. In my role as PIC, I led the HIMTI Region Alam Sutra members, ensuring the smooth and orderly progression of the event, culminating in its successful completion.

  • TECHNO 2022

    • HIMTI Techno 2022 is a welcoming party for freshmen in the computer science department at Binus University. During this event, freshmen are equipped with introductory materials before starting their lectures, such as programming with C. The program also includes various performances from student organizations, such as dance and band performances. This not only provides a more in-depth insight into computer science studies at Binus University but also strengthens the sense of community among computer science students.

    In the HIMTI Techno 2022 event, I was assigned to Division 3, known as DnD, which stands for Design and Development. My role involved generating ideas and presenting my proposed concepts to all Techno 2022 members before implementing them into the final product to contribute to the success of the Techno 2023 event.In addition to my creative contributions within the DnD division, this experience provided me with valuable insights into presentation skills, public speaking, and effective communication in a team setting. Dealing with various aspects of ideation and implementation enhanced my abilities in project management and collaboration.
