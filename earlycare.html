Early Care

Company Profile and E-commerce Website for Early Care

What is Early Care ?

EarlyCare is a beauty company specializing in the production of facial soaps for children, teenagers, and adults. Our product, Early Care Gentle Kids and Teens Facial Foam, is specifically formulated for individuals with sensitive skin. It is free from PARABENS and SLS, contains no artificial fragrances, infused with natural oils, and maintains a balanced pH level. Additionally, it is registered with the Food and Drug Administration (BPOM).

The technology behind the Early Care website

The technology behind the Early Care Company Profile and E-commerce Website is WordPress, utilizing a MySQL database. The user interface is designed using Elementor. For the e-commerce section, WooCommerce is employed. To ensure the website is easily discoverable, EarlyCare relies on Yoast SEO.

What am I working on ?

Building the user interface for the Early Care Company Profile and E-commerce Website, setting up product listings on the website, optimizing the website for efficiency, and correcting errors from the previous design.
